01 / The Eduba frame

A methodology that plugs into trustee ROI, not around it.

The idea is simple. About 60% of any association's operations is database and code (member directory, event registration, trustee billing). About 30% is rule-based logic (dues gates, chapter rules). Around 10% is where a real AI problem lives. Member-to-member commerce measurement, trustee outcome reporting, and an AI-literacy event series for members in tech-heavy verticals are where a methodology partner earns a seat.

The shape we would propose is Eduba listed as a preferred AI trustee or partner, a quarterly AI-literacy event series co-delivered into growth-hub meetings, and a short co-authored piece on AI adoption among Florida veteran-owned businesses that gives the advocacy calendar a content asset to use in Tallahassee.

Nothing added to the FAVOB staff workload. Everything rides the existing cadence.
